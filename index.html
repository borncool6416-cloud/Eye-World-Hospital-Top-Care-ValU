Designer Mission

Media Buyer Mission

This is a dual-funnel strategy. Funnel 1 creates the entry; Funnel 2 closes the sale.

Campaign A: Consultation Leads

Objective: Messages / Leads

Ad Sets: Pair-based (Eye-Dental, Dental-Derma, etc.)

Audience: Cairo + Giza, 22-45 (Women-focused for Beauty, Mixed for Eyes)

Primary: Instagram Reels first, then TikTok.

Campaign B: ValU Conversion

Objective: Traffic / Conversion

Target: Installment-sensitive users, premium healthcare interest.

Strategy: Retarget anyone who engaged with Campaign A but didn't book.

Primary: Meta-led with heavy TikTok support.
