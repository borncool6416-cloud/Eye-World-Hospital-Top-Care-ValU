Eye World Hospital × Top Care × ValU

Eye World Top Care ValU Partnership

My Master Recommendation

"Use Eye World as the performance engine, Dr. Ihab as the authority amplifier, Top Care as the premium destination, and ValU as the conversion unlock."

Launch Non-Negotiables

• Do not merge both campaigns into one ad.

• Do not mix ValU visuals with Top Care/Eye World styles.

• Do not rely on Top Care page alone for scale in week 1.

• Do not launch paid traffic before testing forms & reception.

• Do not promise free treatment—only free consultation.

• Do not use unapproved medical claims or B/A content.

10-Day Daily Execution Plan

DAY 01

Offer Lock + Funnel Design

Owners: Marketing Lead, Media Buyer, Ops, Content Lead

  • Finalize exact wording, specialty matrix, and lead handling SOP.
  • Output: One-page launch sheet + approved CTAs.
DAY 02

Tracking + Infrastructure

Owners: Media Buyer, Web/Admin, Reception Lead

  • Set up Pixel/CAPI, UTMs, WhatsApp routing, and CRM workflow.
  • Output: Live tracking stack + tested lead destination.
DAY 03

Production Day

Owners: Content Team, Designer, Branch Ops

  • Film authority clips, patient journeys, facility tours, and ValU explainers.
  • Output: Raw content bank for 2 weeks.
DAY 04

Creative Build

Build first batch of statics, carousels, and partnership reveals.

DAY 05

Final Approvals

Finalize Arabic captions, subtitles, and disclaimer placements.

DAY 06-07

Organic Preheat & Paid Launch

Owners: Social Media Manager, Media Buyer

Start organic authority posts on Eye World/Dr. Ihab. Launch awareness & message ads on Day 7.

Output: First paid data within 24 hours.

Optimization & Conversion Push (Days 8-10)

Day 8: Review Build retargeting audiences for non-booked leads and video viewers.
Day 9: Push Launch urgency stories and UGC. Reception actively offers ValU finance.
Day 10: Scale Cut weak ads. Prepare 14-day scaling report and next phase plan.

Final Hand-ins (End of Day 10)

Designer

Branded asset library, source files, and branch-ready signage.

Media Buyer

Dashboard with lead quality, winning audiences, and 14-day scaling budget.

Content Team

Sorted content bank, top performing hooks, and 7-day organic follow-up plan.
